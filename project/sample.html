Sample Lesson: The ADDIE Model

Understanding the 5 Stages

The ADDIE model is the gold standard for creating effective training and educational curricula. It consists of five essential stages:

  • Analysis [00:00:33]: Identifying training needs, objectives, and the target audience.
  • Design [00:01:33]: Creating blueprints, storyboards, and logical segments for the content.
  • Development [00:02:14]: Building, assembling, and testing the actual training materials.
  • Implementation [00:03:00]: Delivering the content to the participants.
  • Evaluation [00:03:21]: Using formative and summative feedback to ensure the training is successful.
